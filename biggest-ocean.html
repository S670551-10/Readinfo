Biggest Ocean in the World

The largest ocean in the world is Pacific Ocean. It is the largest and deepest of Earth's ocean basins, covering about 165.25 million square kilometres or approximately 30% of the planet's total surface area. All of the world continents could fit info the Pacific basins.

  • Key Facts about Pacific Ocean

    • Size : It is the largest of the Earth's five oceans by the both voulme and surface.

    Depth : It is also the deepest ocean, containing the Mariana Trench, which is the deepest point on Earth.

    Location : The Pacific Ocean is located between the continents of Asia and Australia on the west and the Americas on the east.

    Geology : The Pacific Ocean is known for being the 'Ring of Fire', an area of intense volcanic and earthquake activity along its tectonic plate boundaries.

    Summary

    The largest ocean in the world is Pacific Ocean. It is largest by both volume and surface area. It is located between the continents of Asia and Australia on the west and Americas on the east.
